TLDR

The project experiments.vol1 is a process to explore ideas in electronic music that are based on interacting voltages.

Interacting Voltages

It seems weird, but sound is represented in our mind as oscillations of voltage. Crazy. When you hear a pitch, say middle C (440hz), some neurons in your brain, prior to cognition, recreate that pitch as a BRAINWAVE, at the same frequency as the pitch you hear1.

That means that when you're at a concert, your brain is literally in harmony with the people around you. Being on the same wavelength suddenly takes on a more literal sense.

Concurrently, music can also be created using voltages, and patterns of voltages. I wonâ€™t get into the technicalities, but this is the basis for analog synthesis.

Process

The classic artistic process. The writers block. From Van Gogh to Salvador Dali, Matisse to Picasso, Mondrian to Lichtenstein, process is critical.

The process that has been developed and employed for this project is that over the period of a year, every idea that was conceived about how voltages could interact was written down on a notes app on a phone. then paper was cut up into a7 segments and each idea with written on one note using a 1970s brother typewriter. This resulted in over 200 cards of inspiration.

This process forms the basis for all experiments projects where experiments.vol1 only scratches the surface of potential.
